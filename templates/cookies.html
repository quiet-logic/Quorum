Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit, such as your login session.

How We Use Cookies

Quorum uses a minimal set of cookies necessary to operate the service:

  • Session cookie — keeps you logged in while you use the app. This is a strictly necessary cookie and cannot be disabled.
  • CSRF token — protects your account from cross-site request forgery attacks. Also strictly necessary.

We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking cookies, or analytics cookies that identify you personally.

Local Storage

In addition to cookies, the Quorum app uses your browser's local storage to remember which study profile you have selected. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, which may set their own cookies:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Stripe sets cookies to detect fraud and ensure secure checkout. See Stripe's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — typography. Fonts are loaded from Google's servers; Google may log requests. See Google's privacy policy.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking the session cookie will prevent you from logging in to Quorum. For other cookies, your browser's help documentation explains how to manage them.

Contact

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at hello@quorumsqe.uk.
