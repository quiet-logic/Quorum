How it works

Study smarter,
not harder.

01
Flip the card

Read the question. Recall the answer. Flip to check. Five card types cover every way the SQE1 tests your knowledge.

02
Rate your recall

Score yourself 0–5. The SM-2 algorithm uses your rating to calculate exactly when you need to see that card again.

03
Review what matters

Cards you struggle with come back sooner. Cards you know well are spaced further apart. No wasted time on what you already know.

04
Sit the exam

Simulate exam conditions with timed 90-question sessions. Track your weak spots across every subject and topic.
